Why partner with Ascent?


Partnering with Ascent helps
organizations up-level their compliance
operations, mapping compliance
requirements to internal controls, policies,
and procedures, and enabling compliance
at every organizational touchpoint.

Empower clients and customers


The Ascent integration removes the need
for manual tracking and analysis of
regulatory websites. Compliance teams
save time and resources as AI-powered
tools automatically identify and flag rules
and rule changes applicable to specific
business units. When regulatory rule
changes occur, obligations are
automatically updated, alerting the user
to assess the impact and take necessary
actions.
