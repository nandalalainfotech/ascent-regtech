Our Approach

Compliance teams of all sizes choose 360 RegTech in order to pinpoint their financial regulatory obligations.

Not just data, but targeted knowledge

Identifying your regulatory obligations is a complicated, time- consuming and costly practice that mires compliance teams in manual work, and must be repeated with every new change in regulation.

This is a problem that can’t be solved by a typical data vendor. Rather than throw mountains of information at you, we use AI to deeply analyze regulatory text to deliver the actual obligations that are relevant to you.

The expertise of a human at the speed of a machine

360 RegTech takes a different approach to automation, and that difference is key to protecting your business from risk. Validated and trained by in-house legal and compliance experts, our world-class algorithms ingest, parse, and analyze millions of lines of regulatory text in order to deliver the insights that matter to you.

The result? A complete inventory of regulatory obligations – targeted to your business, detailed down to the line of regulation, and always up-to-date.


Your implementation partner, every step of the way

Too many technology vendors set it, then forget it. At 360 RegTech, we know that even more important than the decision to purchase a new tool is how effectively you can implement it.

That’s why our Support team works with you hand in hand to ensure your team is properly onboarded and trained, with your goals in mind. As your partner in compliance, we help you get up and running in days, not months.
