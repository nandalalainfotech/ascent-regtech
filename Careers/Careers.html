Charitable Matching Gift Program


Ascent is committed to supporting and encouraging its employees’ contributions to charitable organizations. To assist employees’ charitable giving and augment the impact of their charitable dollars, Ascent created a Charitable Matching Gift Program. The Charitable Matching Gift Program enables Ascent employees to support important organizations they are passionate about with the company providing a match, up to $100 annually for each employee to the charity/charities of their choice. All employee donations to public charities and public educational institutions that are 501(c)(3) are eligible for matching.
